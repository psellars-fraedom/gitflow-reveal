Clear, Defined Branching Strategy

Teams @ Fraedom expect strategies to:

  • be non-intrusive
  • be simple to comprehend
  • be easy to follow
  • be clear and conscise
  • provide support and advice
Delivery teams need a clear, defined branching strategy enabling them to develop across multiple versions of the product simultaneously that is also synchronised with the Fraedom deployment schedule.

Multiple Versions of The Product Simultaneously

Teams @ Fraedom need the ability to:

  • devlop features for the next release
  • fix issues with an upcoming release
  • fix issues that are found in production
  • work in an appropriate level of isolation
  • merge changes with a minimum of fuss across versions
Delivery teams need a clear, defined branching strategy enabling them to develop across multiple versions of the product simultaneously that is also synchronised with the Fraedom deployment schedule.

Synchronised with the Fraedom Deployment Schedule

Teams @ Fraedom must provide support for:

  • 4/5 releases spread across the calendar year
  • regression testing at the end of a release period
  • regular PPM releases
